Questions often seen:


  • Why learn coding?

    At the moment, the demand for programmers with a style is extremely high around the globe. It means there is work for anyone in the free market.


  • Where to start? Which language should you choose?

    There’s certainly no shortage of options. The majority of people recommends JavaScript or Python for beginners. Two possibilities are viable currently. Front- and Back-End. I’m more familiar with the first.


  • Which one is more suitable for you?

    I’d recommend having a taste of both, to see which one fits you better. Eventually both should be part of your knowledge, since a programmer with style is FULL-Stack, meaning you are familiar with each one. There’s a lot of room for experimenting, so take your chance.


  • Why start with web-development?

    Most fellow programmers are lone wolves, since just as like you, the google is our second-best friend, after dogs. It means, colleges around expect you to dig deep in searching for answers, before you ask away.
